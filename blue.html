Color #3: Blue

Blue is my 3rd favorite color. As green was related to nature, blue represents both the sky and the sea. The color invokes rest and freedom. In many cultures, it is seen as a sign of peace or used to keep bad spirits away. Too much of the color can make it invoke sadness or negativity. Too little of it could be related to depression or suspicion. I personally like it due to it reminding me of the sea and water in general. It helps me think of trips I have had in beautiful areas full of nature such as beaches, rivers, or waterfalls. I also like it due to its relaxing vibes. No matter where you are in the world, blue is always there. Either in the sky, sea, flowers, or other objects. Also, my favorite fruit is blueberries so the color also makes me hungry at times.
