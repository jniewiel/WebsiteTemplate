From researching all three of my favorite colors, I found the color black to be the most interesting. I never realized how universal the color is in everything we use. It might even be the most used color in the world in terms of products and style. I personally didn't know about the tradition of widows wearing black for almost their entire lives after the funeral. Even though this tradition isn't carried on as long as it was back then, but it was interesting that it was like this back in history. I also found the color to be interesting due to the fact that it is the only color that is completely absent of light.
